About Us

At Peñaranda National High School, they are committed to offering a supportive and welcoming learning environment that encourages academic performance, individual development, and lifelong learning. You can look around our school using our virtual tour and get a sense of the special opportunities and active community that characterize it.

We hope that this virtual tour will give you an idea of the rich tapestry of academic and personal development that defines PNHS, whether you are a prospective student, parent, or simply an interested visitor.

The Creators

This project is a requirement for Computer Programming class, it was made by Group 2 of the Babbage class 2022-2023. Through this project, we hope to demonstrate the technical knowledge, problem-solving ability, and effective collaboration that are necessary for success in the computer programming industry.
